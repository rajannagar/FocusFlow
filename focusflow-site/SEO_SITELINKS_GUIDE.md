# How to Get Google Sitelinks for Your Website

## What Are Sitelinks?

Sitelinks are the additional links that appear below your main search result in Google, like you see for Apple. They help users navigate directly to important pages on your site.

## What You Need to Do Next

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console
- Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
- Add your property: `https://focusflowbepresent.com`
- Verify ownership (DNS, HTML file, or meta tag)
- Submit your sitemap: `https://focusflowbepresent.com/sitemap.xml`

### 2. Ensure Good Internal Linking

- ✅ Features page

### 3. Build Authority & Traffic
- Get backlinks from other websites
- Share on social media
- Get featured in app directories
- Write blog posts or content
- Get reviews and mentions

### 4. Wait for Google to Index
- Sitelinks are **automatically generated** by Google
- They typically appear after:
  - Your site has been indexed for several weeks/months
  - You have consistent traffic
  - Google recognizes your site structure
  - Your pages are well-linked internally

### 5. Optimize Page Structure
- Use clear, descriptive page titles
- Have unique, valuable content on each page
- Ensure fast page load times
- Make sure pages are mobile-friendly

## Important Notes

⚠️ **You Cannot Force Sitelinks**
- Google decides which sitelinks to show
- They're based on:
  - Site structure
  - User behavior
  - Page importance
  - Internal linking

⏱️ **Timeline**
- Sitelinks usually appear 2-6 months after your site is well-established
- They may appear sooner if your site gets significant traffic

## Tips to Improve Chances

1. **Keep navigation consistent** - Don't change your main menu frequently
2. **Use descriptive anchor text** - "Features" is better than "Click here"
3. **Maintain site hierarchy** - Clear parent/child page relationships
4. **Update content regularly** - Fresh content signals an active site
5. **Monitor in Search Console** - Check for any crawl errors
