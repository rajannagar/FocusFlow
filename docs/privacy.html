Privacy Policy

Effective date:
Contact: Info@softcomputers.ca

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusFlow – Be Present (“FocusFlow”, “the app”) handles information. Soft Computers (“we”, “us”) is the developer of FocusFlow.

1) Summary

  • Guest Mode: your data is stored locally on your device.
  • Signed-in Mode (optional): your data may be synced to a cloud backend to support backup and multi-device use.
  • We do not sell personal information and the app is not ad-supported.
  • We do not require photo library access (avatars are symbol-based).

2) Data the app stores

Depending on how you use FocusFlow, we may store:

  • Account data (Signed-in Mode only): email address (if provided), display name, and authentication identifiers needed to keep you signed in.
  • Focus data: session duration, timestamps, and optional intention text; plus derived stats such as totals and streaks.
  • Habit data: habit names, schedules/reminders, and completion/check-ins.
  • Settings & preferences: themes, goals, reminder preferences, and sound/haptic settings.

3) Where your data is stored

  • Guest Mode: on your device only.
  • Signed-in Mode: synced to a cloud backend used to provide the service (e.g., authentication and database hosting).

We use access controls designed to isolate each user’s data and prevent mixing between accounts.

4) What we do not collect

  • We do not request access to your photos, contacts, or precise location.
  • We do not sell personal information.

5) How we use data

  • To provide the app’s features (focus sessions, habits, stats, achievements, personalization).
  • To sync your data when you choose to sign in.
  • To respond to support requests and improve reliability.

6) Sharing

We share data only when necessary to operate the service (for example, cloud infrastructure providers), or when required by law. We do not sell personal information.

7) Diagnostics

iOS may generate diagnostics such as crash logs depending on your device settings. If diagnostics are available to us, we use them only to troubleshoot and improve app stability.

8) Retention

  • Guest Mode: data remains until you delete the app or reset data inside the app.
  • Signed-in Mode: synced data is retained to provide the service unless you request deletion.

9) Your requests

You may request access or deletion of account-associated data by emailing Info@softcomputers.ca. We may ask you to verify account ownership.

10) Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Questions? Contact Info@softcomputers.ca.
