Privacy

FocusFlow is designed to minimize data collection and respect your privacy. Use the app in Guest Mode without an account, or sign in to enable secure sync.

  • Guest Mode: all data stays on your device
  • Signed-in: sync sessions, tasks, and settings (optional)
  • No photo access required (symbol-based avatars)
  • No ads, no tracking, no data selling
  • Delete your account anytime from within the app
